Here are three ways to explain your project, depending on who you are talking to.

Option 1: The "One-Liner" (For casual conversation)
"I built PropertyHub, a full-stack web application that helps landlords manage their properties and tenants easily, replacing messy paperwork with a digital dashboard."

Option 2: The Professional Pitch (For Interviews & Recruiters)
"What is your project?" "My project is called PropertyHub. It is a comprehensive Property Management System designed to bridge the gap between landlords and tenants. It streamlines the entire rental process, from onboarding tenants to tracking monthly rent payments."

"What is the use?" "The main goal is to automate the daily tasks of property management. Usually, landlords track everything in Excel sheets or WhatsApp messages, which is chaotic. PropertyHub centralizes everything:

For Landlords: It gives a clear financial overview and helps manage maintenance issues efficiently.

For Tenants: It provides a professional portal to check their lease status, pay rent, and request repairs without endless phone calls."
